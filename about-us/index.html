Championing Brands & Branding in the Digital & Technological World Ignite a Passion, Build a Community, Create a Legacy

The 21st-century landscape has ushered in a new era of digital transformation, revolutionising how we connect, communicate, and conduct business. Today, more than ever, branding has become more than just representation; it's about creating meaningful experiences that deeply resonate with audiences.

The Laureate League (TLL), the technology arm of The World Brands Foundation (TWBF), is an innovative company focused on building a digital ecosystem that empowers brands and personalities to thrive in the digital space. By integrating the best of branding, technology, networking, and education, we pave the way for our clients to break new ground.

Built to empower, The Laureate League pioneers in providing exclusive Award NFTs for The BrandLaureate Awards recipients. We have also evolved into an innovative training hub, offering comprehensive, industry-specific Cybersecurity Awareness e-Training Services as well as hosting a line-up of training events, exhibitions, forums, and consultancy services designed to foster growth and equip brands in this digital age.

About our parent company

The BrandLaureate

The World Brands Foundation (TWBF) was established in 2005 as the worlds premier branding foundation with the aim to heighten branding practices and inculcate good brand culture in the arena of business. The BrandLaureate Awards was set up to meet said objectives of the TWBF enabling the Foundation to execute its goals effectively.

Over the past 19 years, The BrandLaureate Awards have become the most prestigious branding awards in the world, with diverse award programs that cater to brands of all sizes and industries. To date, the Awards reach extends across 80 countries, recognizing over 2,500 brands and 500 international brand personalities.
